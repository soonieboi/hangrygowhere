About Hangry-Go-Where

Cannot decide what to eat and where to dine? Spoilt for choice?

Hangry-Go-Where is your go-to platform for discovering exciting food places around you. It is THE solution to all the random bickering and disagreements on where to eat... Whether you're craving sushi, pizza, burgers, or exploring new cuisines, I've got you covered. My mission is to make your food selection journey easy and fuss-free. Just let this app decide!

You'll find a user-friendly interface to roll get a reccomended food place. If you craving for something, use the search for food places and explore more options. The curated list of top-rated places ensures that you have a memorable dining experience every time. Do look out for the ratings~

Hangry-Go-Where is powered by the Google Places API, offering reliable and up-to-date information about restaurants, cafes, and more. Choosing and deciding on a food place is NOT that tough... #solving #firstworldproblems
